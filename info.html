Information regarding Accessibility

We are committed to providing an inclusive and accessible environment for all attendees at our conference. The conference center is equipped with facilities to ensure disabled access and a positive experience for individuals with various accessibility needs. Here are some of the features we have in place:

  • Wheelchair Accessibility: The conference center is wheelchair accessible, with ramps and elevators available for easy navigation between floors.
  • Accessible Restrooms: Accessible restroom facilities are available throughout the venue, equipped to meet the needs of individuals with mobility challenges.
  • Assistance and Support: Our staff members are trained to assist attendees with disabilities. If you require any assistance during the event, please don't hesitate to approach any staff member or visit the information desk.
  • Reserved Seating: Designated accessible seating areas are available in event spaces to accommodate attendees with mobility devices.
  • Service Animals: Service animals are welcome at the conference center. Please ensure they are appropriately leashed or harnessed.
  • Communication Access: We strive to make our event information accessible to everyone. If you require specific accommodations for communication access (e.g., sign language interpreters), please contact us in advance.

Contact for Further Information:

If you have specific questions about disabled access or need further information to plan your visit, please contact our Accessibility Coordinator. We are here to ensure that everyone can fully participate in and enjoy the event.
